Basudebpur Haripriya Institution
Course Of Study
Basudebpur Haripriya Institution prepares students for the Madhyamik Pariksha (Bengali as first Language) conducted by the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education and Uccha Madhyamik conducted by the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education. The medium of instruction is English.
