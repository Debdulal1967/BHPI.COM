Overview
BASUDEVPUR H.P.INSTITUTION was established in 1940 and it is managed by the Department of Education. It is located in Rural area. It is located in EGRA -II block of PURBA MEDINIPUR district of West Bengal. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Bengali is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
Course Of Study
BASUDEVPUR H.P.INSTITUTION prepares students for the Madhyamik Pariksha (Bengali as first Language) conducted by the West Bengal Board of Secondary Education and Uccha Madhyamik conducted by the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education. The medium of instruction is English.
